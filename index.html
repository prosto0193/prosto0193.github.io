POS and m-POS terminals

EMI Global Corp is engaged in the implementation of POS and m-POS terminals for your business.

POS terminals and mobile cash registers are devices for accepting card payments (EMV Chip & PIN cards, magnetic stripe cards, contactless cards, NFC gadgets) for all types of mobile commerce, online stores, courier services, retail outlets, as well as for the service sector.

Our cash registers and mobile cash registers are equipped with everything necessary for any type of trade:sales, inventory control, discounts. The basic software is free. The terminals comply with 54-FZ:it connects to any fiscal data operator and transfers data to the Federal Tax Service.

SoftPOS technology

SoftPOS technology is an innovative solution for accepting contactless payments on users' smartphones without any peripherals. System allows to use mobile device instead of Cash register or Pos-terminal to accept bank cards and QR codes.

Main advantages of SoftPOS over POS (or m-POS) terminals:
Reduce the cost of buying or renting additional equipment.
Reduced equipment maintenance costs.
Minimum time between the purchase of a product and the start of its operation, without involvement of additional specialists
